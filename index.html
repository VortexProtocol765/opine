OPINE.com is an opinion-sharing site that mostly focusses on how people opine on various issues of what is happening in the present world. People can post their opinion (dictum) or create polls regarding any topic based on a set of categories with multiple sub-categories. They can also like, dislike or comment (remark) on any of the dictums of people as well as vote on their created polls. Considering the number of people interaction with the dictums or polls the top-rated posts are displayed in categories likes most liked, most voted, most remarked or most hyped (having a considerable number of remarks/votes and likes). The users can also contact OPINE for any sort of query, criticism or suggestions.

This Page mostly focusses on

    1. Sharing of opinions from all walks of life
    2. Remarks of various people on opinions
    3. Collection of data through polls
    4. Allow specific audience to interact with your posts
    5. Opinions are classified in various categories and sub-categories
    6. Attach media files to opinions (Dictums) and polls.
    Gain knowledge from the discussion on various topics.
